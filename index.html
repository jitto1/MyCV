About Me

I am Jitto Thomas, a Systems Engineer and aspiring Cybersecurity professional with a strong foundation in computer science and web development. My journey in the tech world began with a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science and Engineering from APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University, where I honed my programming and problem-solving skills. Currently, I am pursuing a Master's degree in Cybersecurity at Saarland University, Germany, to deepen my expertise in securing digital infrastructures and combating cyber threats. Over the years, I have cultivated hands-on experience in developing and deploying robust software solutions. At Infosys Limited, I worked as a Systems Engineer, contributing to client projects by developing and enhancing web applications using Angular, ASP.NET MVC, and the .NET framework. My role involved creating dynamic front-end interfaces and efficient back-end systems while leveraging technologies like SQL and LINQ to manage and manipulate data effectively. Collaborating closely with teams and clients, I developed a knack for delivering tailored software solutions that meet diverse business needs. My technical expertise spans multiple programming languages, including C, Python, Java, and C++, along with a solid understanding of web technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I am proficient in frameworks such as Entity Framework, .NET Core, and ASP.NET MVC, which enable me to design scalable and maintainable web applications. My enthusiasm for cybersecurity has driven me to earn certifications like the EC-Council Certified Secure Computer User, reflecting my commitment to safeguarding data and systems in today's digital landscape. What sets me apart is my ability to blend technical proficiency with a creative mindset. Whether it's brainstorming with a team to devise innovative solutions or independently coding complex systems, I am driven by a passion for learning and a desire to create meaningful impacts through technology. As I continue my journey, my goal is to leverage my skills and experiences to contribute to innovative projects that drive technological advancements while ensuring the security and integrity of digital systems. If you are looking for someone with a unique combination of technical skills, creativity, and dedication, I am eager to collaborate and make a difference.
